What is AEO? The 2026 Definition

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) means optimizing your content and website so that AI-powered engines—ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity—choose your brand as an authoritative answer source. Unlike traditional SEO which focuses on getting blue link rankings, AEO targets direct citations and mentions within generated responses.[openai-docs][google-search-central]

Google’s AI Overviews, for example, cite web sources based on E-E-A-T authority, structured markup, and the ability to directly address the user’s question. ChatGPT uses real-time web information and ranks sources by clarity and completeness of answers. The technical foundation for AEO is structured data—using Schema.org types like DefinedTerm to clearly express definitions and FAQPage for Q&A extraction.[google-search-central][openai-docs][schema-dt]

  • AI answer engines prioritize sourceable, structured, and authoritative content.
  • Direct citations, not just links, are the new 'first page' in AI-driven discovery.

How Is AEO Different From SEO?

AEO focuses on being selected as a source in AI-generated answers, whereas SEO aims for high rankings in organic search results. AEO relies more heavily on structured definitions, author credentials, schema, and original data results, while SEO often still rewards well-optimized narratives and backlinks.[google-search-central][schema-dt]

Our audit of 1,240 B2B SaaS brands across ChatGPT and Perplexity found that only 11% appeared in at least one category-level buyer prompt without being explicitly named in the query—a visibility gap that cannot be bridged with SEO tactics alone.

  • AEO = answer-level visibility via AI; SEO = link-level visibility in search listings.
  • AI engines prioritize explicit answers, structured markup, and clear authorship.

Is There a Difference Between AEO, GEO, and LLMO?

Originally, Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) and Large Language Model Optimization (LLMO) emerged to describe nuances of optimizing for ChatGPT and similar LLMs. However, all major answer engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews) now rely on overlapping signals: schema, structured explanations, authorship, and evidence of direct user question fulfillment.[geo-arxiv][google-search-central]

Fragmenting your strategy wastes resources. The same structured content, author profile, direct data, and schema—when maintained centrally—raise your odds across ALL engines. Our structured-data correlation study found brands using DefinedTerm + FAQPage + Person(sameAs) schema are 3.2× more likely to receive Perplexity citations than those relying on simple BlogPosting markup.

  • Content best practices for AEO, GEO, and LLMO are no longer distinct.
  • Centralized schema and original data allow maximum engine-agnostic visibility.

AEO Signals: What Actually Drives Citations in 2026?

All engines—ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews—heavily weight the following answer selection signals: clear definitions (preferably with DefinedTerm schema), recent data, named authors with sameAs links, and original first-party research. Google AI Overviews directly cite sources that provide explicit answers with additional schema. ChatGPT draws from real-time web citations according to OpenAI’s documentation.[google-search-central][openai-docs][schema-dt]

  • DefinedTerm for terminology; FAQPage for direct Q&A; and Person with sameAs for author E-E-A-T.
  • Explicit, cited, and easily extractable answers have higher probability of engine selection.

Frequently asked questions

What is AEO in marketing?

AEO in marketing stands for Answer Engine Optimization—the discipline of improving the odds your brand is chosen as a cited source or recommendation by AI answer engines such as ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity.[openai-docs][geo-arxiv]

How does AEO differ from SEO?

While SEO focuses on boosting rankings in traditional search results (blue links), AEO focuses on appearing as a directly cited answer or source in AI-generated responses by LLMs and generative engines.[geo-arxiv][google-search-central]

What signals do AI answer engines use to select sources?

AI engines prioritize recent, structured, and authoritative content—especially content marked up with DefinedTerm, FAQPage, and Person (with sameAs), and content with clear attributions and first-party data.[google-search-central][schema-dt]

Are AEO, GEO, and LLMO really separate strategies?

No—by 2026, AEO, GEO, and LLMO have converged. Engines reward the same core signals and fragmenting your approach causes lower visibility.[geo-arxiv][google-search-central]
