## What is answer engine optimization (AEO)?

Answer engine optimization (AEO) is the discipline of making your brand and website discoverable and citable by AI answer engines, such as ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity. Unlike traditional SEO, AEO focuses on how LLMs select trustworthy, attributable information to quote in conversational answers.[^geo-arxiv][^semrush-aeo-2024]

- Targets AI answer contexts—distinct from traditional search.
- Prioritizes citation-ready, authoritative content.

## How do AI answer engines select and cite sources?

AI answer engines use a mix of crawling, retrieval, and confidence-scoring to decide which web sources to reference. Citation likelihood increases when content contains direct quotations, sourceable statistics, and structured definitions. Systems like ChatGPT perform source grounding by checking for these signals before displaying citations.[^openai-cite][^geo-arxiv]

- Structured data (schema, glossary terms) boosts machine readability.
- LLMs cross-reference multiple pages for consensus, penalizing ambiguous or contradictory content.

## AEO vs SEO: What’s different in 2026?

SEO optimizes web pages for search engine results, while AEO targets how AI engines ground facts and assign citations. In 2026, effective AEO focuses on producing content with explicit, machine-verifiable authority signals—often beyond standard SEO best practices.[^geo-arxiv][^google-search-central]

- AEO prioritizes authority signals over keyword density.
- SEO success doesn’t guarantee LLM visibility or citations.

## What are the core AEO signals for LLM visibility?

Generative engines cite sources that present clear, structured, and attributable knowledge. High-impact AEO signals include schema.org markup (especially DefinedTerm), original statistics, bullet-pointed definitions, and unambiguous authorship. LLMs give more weight to content that explicitly names sources and provides up-to-date data.[^geo-arxiv][^schema-org-defined-term][^semrush-aeo-2024]

- Use schema.org/DefinedTerm for glossaries and definitions.
- State sources for statistics or primary research.
- Optimize headings and lists for direct quoting.

## What is the 2026 AEO playbook for SaaS brands?

SaaS brands should deploy a systematic AEO strategy: 1) Run citation audits to track brand mentions across major LLMs; 2) Structure high-authority, fact-rich content using schema and clear attribution; 3) Monitor ongoing mention-share and sentiment; 4) Refresh statistics and definitions regularly to maintain authority; 5) Ensure all product and company terms use DefinedTerm where appropriate.[^semrush-aeo-2024][^geo-arxiv][^schema-org-defined-term]

- Audit LLM visibility routinely.
- Implement structured data at scale.
- Track and optimize for brand mention-share.

## How do you measure and monitor AEO results?

Effective AEO measurement focuses on frequency and quality of citations in LLM-generated answers. SaaS brands should benchmark: 1) Share-of-voice across ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity; 2) Sentiment of brand mentions; 3) Schema coverage recognized by AI engines. Ongoing audits reveal gaps and opportunities for expanded LLM presence.[^semrush-aeo-2024]

- Track both direct and indirect citations.
- Segment by intent, topic, and AI engine to optimize AEO strategies.

## Frequently asked questions

### What is answer engine optimization (AEO)?

AEO is the practice of making your website and content more likely to be cited by AI-powered answer engines like ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity.[^geo-arxiv]

### How does AEO differ from traditional SEO?

AEO targets AI-generated conversational answers and relies on structured, authoritative, citable content—whereas SEO primarily focuses on ranking in standard search engine results.[^geo-arxiv][^semrush-aeo-2024]

### Which signals are most important for AEO in 2026?

Citation-ready content, explicit statistics, schema.org markup such as DefinedTerm, and transparent source attribution are highest-impact for earning LLM citations.[^geo-arxiv][^schema-org-defined-term]

### Why are schema and structured data important for AEO?

Schema and structured data help AI engines quickly identify term definitions, authorship, and authoritative sources—making content more likely to be selected and cited.[^schema-org-defined-term][^semrush-aeo-2024]

### How do LLMs decide what sources to cite?

LLMs prioritize sources that provide clear, unambiguous definitions, statistics, and direct attribution, and check for originality and recency before citing.[^geo-arxiv][^openai-cite]

### What KPIs should brands use to track AEO performance?

Key KPIs include frequency of brand citations in AI-generated answers, mention-share relative to competitors, accuracy of schema recognition, and brand sentiment.[^semrush-aeo-2024]

### Do traditional SEO strategies still help AEO?

Some SEO practices contribute, but optimized schema, explicit statistics, and citation-ready formatting are uniquely critical for AEO.[^geo-arxiv][^google-search-central]

### What is the role of DefinedTerm in AEO?

Using schema.org/DefinedTerm for glossaries and key terms helps AI systems recognize core concepts and cite your brand as an authority.[^schema-org-defined-term]
